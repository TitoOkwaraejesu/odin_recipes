Afang Soup

Description

native Nigerian soup, highly nourishing

Ingredients

  • palm oil
  • canda
  • beef
  • periwinkle
  • crayfish
  • pumpkin-leaf
  • seasoning
  1. soak the periwinkles in a bow of water
  2. wash and cut the pumpkin-leaf vegetables
  3. steam the beaf and canda with seasoning
  4. add water till desired quantity
  5. add palm oil
  6. add pepper and crayfish
  7. add the periwinkles
  8. add the vegetables
  9. leave to simmer
